Miscellaneous posts that I moved from Medium

During 2017 and 2018, I posted most of what I wrote — fiction, book discussion, a series of posts about aphantasia, and miscellaneous blog-style posts — on Medium. I’m about to delete my Medium account. Earlier this year, I moved to this site a baker’s dozen of miscellaneous pieces that I wanted to keep.

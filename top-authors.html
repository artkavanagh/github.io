Top 12 authors

I began my email newsletter, Talk about books, in November 2020 and I’ve sent out new posts approximately every two weeks since then. So far, there have been 108 posts. There are some authors that I’ve returned to frequently, others that I’ve written about only once but may revisit in future. Listed below are the twelve authors about whom I’ve written most. I’ve included posts that appear here on my personal site, either because I wrote them before I started Talk about books or in a few cases because they are further comments on something I’ve already written about in the newsletter.
